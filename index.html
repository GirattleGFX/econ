About Econ

Econ is a economy based Discord Bot with over 50+ commands and counting! What Econ has to offer is a fully customizable economy system, free to use in your Discord server! We created econ because we wanted to push the limits of the BDFD (Bot Designer for Discord) app, to make a bot that is just right for anyone who wishes to use it.

All Commands

e!set - Allows you to customize anything on the bot. Use e!help to learn more.

e!reset - Resets everything on the bot back to default settings. Gems will not be affected using this command.

e!add-coins - Allows you to add coins to a user in your server.

e!remove-coins - Allows you to remove coins from a user in your server.

e!shop - Allows you to view the server's shop!

e!buy - Allows you to buy any item in that server's shop.

e!profile - Shows you your (or another user's) profile for the bot. Including badges, gems, money for that server, etc.

e!bal - Allows you to view how much money you have in your wallet, bank, and all money in totle.

e!daily - Collect your daily income.

e!work - Work every hour to earn money in that server.

e!job-list - Allows you to view the list of jobs available in that server.

e!job-set - Allows you to set your job so you can start working.

e!beg - Beg for money.

e!hunt - Hunt for animals, and sell them later on.

e!fish - Go fishing, bring back food for your family.

e!shed - View what animals you have hunted.

e!inv - View what items you have in your invatory.
